UPDATES ON MONDAYS

Page 33

posted 3 May 2021
Since I'm studying this holiday's origins for school, have an early Happy Cinco de Mayo! This page is a reminder that Valentine does not like hands being stuck in his face (reference Page 13, Panel 6 and Page 14, Panels 5-6). This is one of the main quirks of this character, as well as his distaste for goggles and other things on his head. Luckily, Cassandra is more of an inclusive person who has both noticed and understood these disinclinations, and would have been nicer about it were it not for the pressure of having to go to Sector Nine. Alastair, however, is more demanding and insensitive, and blatantly disregards the feelings of others. And he's the Director.
I finally canonized his name! Now I can call him James in the commentary! Such relief. I'm likely not going to go over the fact that most people call him James instead of Juniper. I did a little research, and while Juniper is categorized as unisex, but a majority of people with that name are female. It's getting more common these days to call people by their middle names. I had a friend back in middle school who was called by their middle name, and I thought, that's pretty cool. Well, there's the whole thing on that. Terry, Ainsley, and Cassandra are the ones who were allowed by Alastair to decide on the name. Like naming a newborn baby. Or a dog. Or a turtle.
