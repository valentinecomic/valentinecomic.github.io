Page 8

posted 28 Dec. 2020
Can this man stay conscious for FIVE MINUTES? Or is that, just, not possible? The populace of the Seventh Dimension were so full of such unorthodox quirks that any experienced transdimensional historian could identify an individual from that realm. This "non-magic cathartic method" is actually significant in the identification of his origins. Fortuitously, we have a history expert in the room! In reality, more than one. First, there's Terry, who has a trove of historical knowledge of the nine dimensions equivalent to or even exceeding her brother's. This is due to their access to the same institutions and materials of study. And! In her introduction, Cass called herself a doctor, but a doctor of what? We'll find out later on, but she does know a lot more about about outsider magic than she lets on. Perhaps less than the Jensen siblings - who study it as a hobby - but nevertheless a substantial amount.
(Off-topic: if you're currently examining my interesting little Aurendalean buildings and thinking, "Wow, these look like tacky little cardboard cutouts," that effect was intentional!)
