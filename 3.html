UPDATES ON MONDAYS

Page 3

posted 7 Dec. 2020
In 1947, the Assembly and the rest of the secret magical organizations were only aware of nine Dimensions, and the 10th was discovered in the late 1980s. These Dimensions are totally unrelated to the actual "ten dimensions" of space (only three of which we are spatially aware) as propagated by the Superstring Theory. Rather, this is just a coincidence of my imagination, meaning that there are still individuals with three-dimensional awareness in the 7th Dimension.
What Terry is alluding to when she says that our dude is a "Seventher" is related to an ancient story that many critics see as simply a myth. Educated as these critics are, most of the commoners (at least, in the Third Dimension) believe in this story because of a very accurate firsthand account that covered all bases regarding the mysteries of the Seventh Dimension.
Also, if you wanted me to describe each one of these peeps, I'd note: Terry is the bookish, studious friend who basically sees anything and goes, "KOWALSKI, ANALYSIS!" and you're dragging her out of the library at 3 AM because enough is enough, my friend. Cass is the funny one who everyone thinks doesn't study at all until she gets the highest score on the final. Ainsley is the quiet but kind one who's always devising something big and cheats on the final. Pretty good friend group.
