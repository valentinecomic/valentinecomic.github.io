UPDATES ON MONDAYS

Page 7

posted 26 Dec. 2020
As expected, handshakes are not a very common method of establishing acquaintanceship anywhere else in the universe, so each newcomer to Earth has to be taught this odd gesture. However, most newcomers can state their name with ease! So why can't this man say his own name? And why does it seem he has developed a tiny crush on Cassandra?
To clarify, what Ainsley feels for Valentine is more like curiosity than actual affection. Externally, this translates to an intense love-at-first-sight front that Cass and Terry go nuts about. On the other hand, what Valentine feels for Cass is in fact admiration. As unstable a person the former is, having been throttled toward Earth, all he understands about this feeling is that it surely is pleasant. But he doesn't know anything about love. In fact, love is still something he regards with an analytical eye. Rather than being the type to pay attention to his feelings, he has more of an inclination to attribute any interest in people to "natural instincts."
Which is true, like, people do naturally want to converge and talk and communicate. People do naturally want to form bonds with each other, which is like the cutest thing about humanity. That is probably why we have civilization/families/etc. So this "pleasant feeling" thing is something we'll be exploring more throughout the plot. Just thought I'd bring that up. :)
