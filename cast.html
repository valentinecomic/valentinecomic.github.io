Cast

Here's a fun little page with all my OCs! Ik it gets a little hard to keep track of comic characters sometimes hehe~

James Valentine

Presently a human, formerly a parasite. Occupies the highest title in Norbury: Fire Chief. He's supposedly cursed by the ancient Charm of Hewin.

Cassie Del Rey

Deputy Chief. Light on her feet, and also wields the coolest axe ever. Life is a game of puzzles and Cassie is the master of solving them.

Samael Callis

Deputy Chief. Hotheaded swordsman/alchemist prodigy who publicly opposes the Fire Chief. He'd do anything to get his sister back from the parasites.

Aegis Callis / "Cupid"

Sam's brother. Or he used to be, until he sided with the parasites during the war. He's kinda emo now and wants to destroy humanity~ womp womp

Luci Callis

Sam's lost sister, and the youngest of the Callis siblings. Carries a bunny stuffed toy that was handmade by Aegis.

Simon Sparkle

The seven-year-old kid that James saved from the fire at Beauregard Orphanage! He's cute so I included him here lol

Tristan Luzon

Also mainly included Tristan here cause he's cute. He's a Lieutenant of the Norbury Defense Department and also a good boi I LOVE HIM-

Aydenn Augustus von Roseus

A fairy dude who works as the Master Healer at the best hospital in Norbury. He looks dead inside but takes a huge interest in James' curse.
