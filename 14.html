Dr. Winifred takes action! Really, she's the only one who doesn't fall apart during emergency situations. Not-so-secretly, I'm so excited to get them out of this room. No offense, Terry, but your bookcase is so hard to color without mixing up the books! I probably should have made a color key. T_T
Some philosophy insight~ I made a very obvious breakthrough yesterday when I realized that a comic will probably have two people speaking in one frame, sometimes even more. But not everyone talks at the same time! Meaning, on the top right, Ainsley and Terry aren't talking over one another - one will shut up while the other is talking. Common sense? So in the comic universe, each character is their own timeline, because somehow it makes sense that two mouths are speaking at the same time. Interesting.
