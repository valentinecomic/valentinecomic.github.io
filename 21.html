Welcome to Cassandra's (and Victoria's) lab! If you're wondering who Victoria is, and whether or not I have introduced her yet, the answer is no. Am I going to introduce her? I will cross that bridge when I get there! Their lab is a really comfy place, and this set will certainly be used for more than just this scene. (Actually... don't read my comic if continuity is your pet peeve... heh...)
So there's a bunch of cool stuff in her lab. Perhaps something that attracted your eye is the lights. Yeah, it's super bright in there, meaning it gets kind of warm. This is so as to account for the required temperature of all the specimens inside.
