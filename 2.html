Page 2

posted 30 Nov. 2020
You know how in those TV series where there's like a whole group of friends, and they all tacitly agree that every time the group has a meeting, it's just ALWAYS going to be in ONE character's house? This is practically the dynamic with Terry, Ainsley, and Cass. Want to discuss lab work? Meeting at Terry's dorm in 5. Want to have a chat over lunch? Meeting at Terry's dorm in 5. Want to drag a shady, blood-soaked, half-dead man in a literal coma somewhere secret? Simple. Meeting at Terry's dorm in 5. Canonically, Terry does have the biggest dorm because of her high social status among those in the AoSF, and there's also a lot more books and materials in there, as well.
It is safe to assume that whoever is reading comic pages would have realized that the title of the webcomic is Valentine, and thus theorized that this must be the name of the main character. It is! Congratulations! Valentine's scars, as well as the tale behind that big slash in the middle, are crucial aspects of the overall story. Feeling as if he is defined by these scars is essentially the only thing that stops him from feeling at home with the AoSF.
Grounds for some gratifying character development, amirite?
