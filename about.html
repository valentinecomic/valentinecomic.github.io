About Valentine

Agent Juniper James Valentine landed on Earth in 1947 with absolutely no knowledge of his past. However, he regards this as more of a clean slate than a tragic loss. Unfortunately, premonitions and hallucinations taunt him into thinking his aura is innately corrupt, despite the consolation of his fellow people at the Supernatural Assembly (better known as the Assocation) that he is a vessel of pureblooded light magic!
Fast forward eighty years, James has experienced a lot of what Earth has to offer, while still being a quiet member of society. In fact, he is so reserved that he has avoided relationships and work commitments. An honorable and intelligent man who does not mind not knowing who he was in the past, James lives a self-satisfied life, even taking pride in his low-profile identity. But a sudden accident paves the way for darkness to return to his soul, threatening to undo the mien of normalcy that James has been keeping up for so long. This new, unwelcome magic within him proves to be so conniving and malevolent that James quickly realizes he cannot fight it alone.
Though reluctant, James must accept the aid of a group of detached students of the Association. However, the more hands that are outstretched toward him, the more deception he faces. In a world where one doesn't know who to trust, will James ever find his way out of his internal dilemma? Filled with altercations between longtime acquaintances, magical battles, and a constantly morphing definition of the difference between right and wrong, there is never a dull moment in the life of Agent Valentine.
