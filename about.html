About Valentine

In the Pacific Islands of Norbury, Juniper James Valentine is one of the most respected Special Operators. His job? Only to vanquish dark spirits when they ambush the islands. (Y'know. Like you do.)
James has experienced a lot of what the AOSF (Assembly of Supernatural Forces has to offer, while still being a quiet member of society. In fact, he is so reserved that he has avoided relationships and work commitments. An honorable and intelligent man who does not mind not knowing who he was in the past, James lives a self-satisfied life, even taking pride in his low-profile identity. But a sudden accident paves the way for darkness to seep into his soul, threatening to undo the mien of normalcy that James has been keeping up for so long. This new, unwelcome magic within him proves to be so conniving and malevolent that James quickly realizes he cannot fight it alone.
Though reluctant, James must accept the aid of a group of detached students at Lucerne Academy. However, the more hands that are outstretched toward him, the more deception he faces. In a world where one doesn't know who to trust, will James ever find his way out of his internal dilemma? Filled with betrayal between longtime acquaintances, magical battles, and a constantly morphing definition of the difference between right and wrong, there is never a dull moment in the life of S.O. Valentine.
