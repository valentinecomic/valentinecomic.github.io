posted 8 Feb. 2021
The Tenebris Congregation is a group that most of the higher-ups at the Association HQ haven't even heard about yet, but they're basically the antithesis of everything the Association stands for. And, Rhea Winifred is none other than Cassandra Winifred's sister! So, this raises the question, why would two siblings deviate like that? Both the Winifreds come from a bloodline of light magic users, so there must be something really enticing about the goal of the Tenebris Congregation that interested Rhea. Perhaps they're not that bad after all...
Rhea is technically Dr. Davis' partner, as they are in the same rank (more details about Tenebris rankings coming soon), but she basically gets dragged around by Dr. Davis while recruiting people from the Assembly. In 1947, Tenebris was still in its recruiting phase. Speaking of which, being a shy pushover of a person myself, I had a small bit of fun with Dr. Davis while establishing him as an incredibly underhandedly rude but refined jerkface.
