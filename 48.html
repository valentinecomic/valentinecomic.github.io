UPDATES ON MONDAYS

Page 48

posted 16 Aug. 2021
Ever write so often that a little groove appears in your finger? Pretty much sums up my ring finger right now. This page is a little less intricate because the words are supposed to carry the page. I do have plans for redrawing this page, but it's not really that bad, now that I'm looking at it. Definitely better than the Prologue art. The next few pages definitely compensate for the lack of design in Alastair's office. Better shading, better outlines.
Roy is the type of person who's really intelligent and wants to help everyone. He's aware of boundaries which he should not overstep, which makes him a prime choice for Alastair when it comes to important cases like Valentine's. Conversely, Alec is sort of brash and lighthearted, and needs to be on a leash. But he still knows how to please Alastair, and though it's only very briefly noted on this page, Alec is very ambitious and actually cares about his fellow agents. Thus, he's the ideal partner for Roy.
I'm aware the nature of the conflict in this page is kind of way past my age, but it's also about characters who are way past my age. Definitely I'm making sure not to show actual drinking. Just enough hinting to make an educational PSA about getting help for emotional problems, you know? But the rating for this thing is probably PG-13, which I'm deciding on noting either on Page 1 or the About page.
