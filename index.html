Everybody's heard of that thing where the antagonist tries to get the protagonist to join their side by offering them freedom or something they really desire. That's what Dr. Davis is trying to do with Benedict. Since they used to be friends, that sly man is using what he knows about his now-distanced companion to his advantage. As Tenebris isn't a well-known organization at the time of this scene, no one really knows that Davis wants to destroy them, nor are they aware that there are at least fifty other people willing to defect from the AoSF.
