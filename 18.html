Challenge: Show Becky's entire personality in one tiny page. Challenge accepted. She's fun, can change the background of the panels she's in at will, and is just totally in love with the meanest person at the Association. Somehow (to me) these colorful backgrounds fit with the overall dark layer under the panels. And it's Dr. Hadley if you're speaking to her formally. So in some way, she touches Alastair's heart! Is it magic or pure goodness? Terry will never know. And Benedict will just cringe as he watches this mushy scene in the background.
