posted 30 Dec. 2020
Interesting looking tree you got there. Be a shame if some soldiers... defaced it! Yes. This is one of the aspects of this project that I had imagined really early on. Take solace in the fact that never once have I thought of naming it the "Enchanted Tree." Such a clich√© would send people reeling. The essence of it is that the tree's really incredible to look at, but if you were to actually walk into the cave, you would want to get the heck out of there. In fact, according to legend, people who happened upon the cave in which the tree resides would actually end up walking out, no questions. Like they went into a room and forgot what they needed.
Sticking with a pinkish but ominous color palette to symbolize the allure of power. It turned out great! Initially the plan was to have soldiers in the picture trying to grab some of the charms sticking out of the tree but I figured that would mess up the whole peace and beauty of it, so I decided, like, not to add that in. (Anyway, my hands were kind of tired from all the layering and texturing, and if I were to actually add those soldiers in, I would probably have thrown my tablet out the window-)
